Team

TAN ZI HAO (editor, illustrator) is a multi-disciplinary conceptual artist, writer, graphic designer, and researcher. He founded Students in Resistance with an ambition to advocate intellectual activism in Malaysia. Through this platform he encourages students to write and critique. His broader academic and artistic interests include the politics of language, Southeast Asian history, art criticism, and anarchism.

YVONNE TAN YIT FONG (editor, writer) is an English literature student on a quest for the narratives in Malaysia. The Chinese secret societies, the legends of princesses, and our everyday epics are just some of them. She is also a sucker for postcolonialism.
